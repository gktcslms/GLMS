How to register and login ?


Click on Sign Up Button

Click on Sign Up button to register your account.

Fill and Submit Registeration Form

Fill the details, then select registraion type and submit the form.

You will get activation email

You will get activation email on your registered email account. Click on Activate Account button, only then your account will be activated.

Click on Login Button

Click on Login Button, after successful registeration and activating your account.

Login to your account

Login to your account by entering registered email id and password

Forgot password.

If you dont remember your password, Click on forgot password button on login form

Reset password.

To reset your password, enter your registered email id, then click on Submit. You will get email with reset password link.
